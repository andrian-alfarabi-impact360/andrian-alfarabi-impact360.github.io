PLAY

Flip the Knife

Swipe up to flip the knife. Make sure it lands correctly to get 3 stars.

Flip continuously to earn a higher score.

Flip it over chairs, tables, pots and pans, bottles, weighing machines, and other household items.

Did we mention this? There’s an annoying bird that tries to sabotage your knife trajectory.

Features:

  • Easy to learn, hard to master
  • Increasingly challenging
  • Upgrade your knives collection. From machetes, to medieval knives, we’ve got em all.
  • Endless gameplay
