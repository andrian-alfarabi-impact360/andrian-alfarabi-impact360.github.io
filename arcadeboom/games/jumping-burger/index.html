You’re a sliding burger with jumping ability.

Avoid obstacles such as ketchup bottles, birds and tiny mice as you slide forward, while collecting ingredients to unlock the perfect burger.

Ingredients include lettuce, tomato, cheese, burger patties, mushroom and salmon fillets.

Grab Chillis to be come temporarily invincible.

How far can you go?

Features:

  • Cute restaurant diner setting
  • Easy to play, hard to master. This game is very challenging.
  • A shop to unlock your burgers
