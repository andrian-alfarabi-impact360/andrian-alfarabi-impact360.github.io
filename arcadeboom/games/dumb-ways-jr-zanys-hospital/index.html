From the creators of Dumb Ways to Die, the hit brand with over 300 million downloads total on Google Play and Apple App Store - we present to you an exciting spin-off brand called Dumb Ways JR Zany’s Hospital.

Visit Dumb Ways Hospital with Doctor Zany. Explore three main areas of play: a waiting room full of surprises to discover, a check-up room where children can play the role of doctor, and an ambulance that visits the hospital featuring fun guest appearances from the original Dumb Ways cast!

This game is perfect for users who love self-exploration and learning by doing.

Features:

  • Perform check-ups on Boffo, Loopy, Madcap and Dumbbell
  • Discover the differences between each character’s heartbeat, blood pressure and more
  • Tap on the characters to see their personality!
  • Enjoy fun surprises – what happens if you leave Boffo on the waiting room chair?
  • Also features three original Dumb Ways characters to play with: Lax, Doofus and Mishap!
