Now, your kids can express themselves through art.

Place your favourite Arty Mouse stickers onto the board, decorate them as you wish.

Drag and drop, resize and reposition them to your hearts content. Need to erase a sticker? No problem.

Create millions of sticker combinations using different Arty Mouse characters, objects and backgrounds. Work with Scribble the dog, Stripy the cat, Dot the bear, Rainbow Birds, Spiro the snail, and more characters.

Features:

  • Simple tutorial to get started
  • Free form activity for kids to encourage creativity
  • Over 120 sticker items ranging from animals, to party hats, glasses, sun, moon, sandcastles, tractors, rockets and more.
  • Fun and positive theme
  • Ability to enlarge the screen for easier previews
