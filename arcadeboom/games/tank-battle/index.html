Two tanks emerge on the battlefield. Only one will survive. Can you outsmart the other tank?

Collect ammo crates and powerups. Navigate your way to the enemy tank. Fire away!

This is the perfect game to play with friends and family.

Tip: For two player mode, play on the same device (one finger on each side). Enhance social interaction of your audience!

Features:

  • Interactive tutorial to learn the ropes
  • Collect different powerups such as additional health, bubble shields and speed boost
  • Randomly generated battlefield. Each terrain is different
  • Intelligent computer AI
  • Singleplayer or Two Player mode
  • Beautiful, low polygon theme
  • Unlimited gameplay
