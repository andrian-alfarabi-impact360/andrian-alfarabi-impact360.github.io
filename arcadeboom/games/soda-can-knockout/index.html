Features:

  • Challenging levels. Encounter different sets with different soda cans
  • Wide landscape (horizontal) design, with fullscreen mode
  • Beautiful 3D carnival theme.
  • Simple and addictive gameplay.
  • Realistic physics

This game is great for fans of various beverage brands such as Coca Cola, Pepsi, Red Bull, Monster and more.
