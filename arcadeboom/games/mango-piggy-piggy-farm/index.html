Mango Piggy Piggy is HUNGRY. He comes across a mango farm. The mangoes look sweet and juicy, just like how he likes it.

Drag and aim to launch Mango Piggy Piggy towards the mangos. Eat them all to earn 3 stars!

Features:

  • Fun, positive and ridiculously cute character. MPP bounces around and squeals almost like a soft toy.
  • Innovative touch and drag mechanic
  • Over 30 levels
  • Unlock a hidden level
  • Don’t eat the red mango! You’ll lose a life
  • Don’t eat the rotten mango! You’ll lose the level
