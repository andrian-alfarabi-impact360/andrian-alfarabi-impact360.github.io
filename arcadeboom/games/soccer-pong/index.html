Soccer Pong

  • Soccer
  • Sports
  • Family
  • Arcade

How good are you at pong? Drag the paddle, and hit the ball into the opponent’s goal post.

Play in a soccer setting with the crowd cheering you all the way. A fun and engaging game for everybody.

Features:

  • 3 different gameplay modes (Easy, Difficult and Expert).
  • Easy mode is the standard classic pong
  • Difficult mode increases the pressure with faster balls and smarter computer
  • Expert mode ( the pong paddle is free to roam everywhere, the ball is fast and the computer is clever!
  • Extremely intuitive controls for mobile (drag anywhere on the screen to move the paddle)
