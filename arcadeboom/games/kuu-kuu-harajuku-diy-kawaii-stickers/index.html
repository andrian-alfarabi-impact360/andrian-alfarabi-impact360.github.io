Tick tock, time to rock! Kuu Kuu Harajuku is an animation brand created by Grammy winner Gwen Stefani. They’re back with a game for tween and teen girls!

Create your own sticker book with characters from the hit TV series. Drag and drop stickers that represent G, Love, Angel, Music and Baby into beautiful backgrounds of Harajuku City. Start with the Clubhouse, and move your way to decorate the studio, parks, and the Harajuku City skyline!

Add fun decorations such as kawaii fruits, donuts, ice cream, burgers and tacos.

Add fun bling such as kawaii headphones, smartphones, disco balls and cute kawaii hand luggage.

Not enough kawaii? Add cute creature stickers such as hamsters, cats, monsters, happy clouds and happy mushrooms. Don’t forget Rudie, the clumsy yet effective talent manager that always gets the girls into troublesome gigs.

On top of that, add cool kawaii vehicles such as segway scooters, the HJ5 tour bus, an ice cream truck, a monster truck, and not to mention, the official HJ5 blimp!

Experience the fun banter between characters while creating your sticker book.

Features:

  • Ability to rotate stickers and scale up or down
  • Interactive tutorial to learn the ropes
  • Save your creations and export to images, directly into your computer or device
  • Messed up your stickers? No worry, there’s a reset button.
  • Extremely fun kawaii theme, suitable for girls
