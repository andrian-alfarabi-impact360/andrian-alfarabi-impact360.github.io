It’s the adventures of Spidey. Shoot webs, grab on and swing like a spider pro. Avoid things such as sharp edges, rotating chainsaws, arrows, mace and other deadly objects.

Keep the majority of your body parts intact, and get to the finish line. Keep our beloved Spidey alive!

Features

  • Touch, keyboard and mouse controls
  • City skyscraper theme
  • Update: Over 48 extremely challenging levels. Test your spider-man skills.
  • Presence of blood and gore (can be turned off by request)
  • Fans of spiderman and marvel superheroes will love the game
