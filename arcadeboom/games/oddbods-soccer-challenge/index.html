Fancy a soccer game with your favourite Oddbods? Kick the ball into the goal, but beware! The mischievous Oddbods are here to stop you.

Features:

  • Compete against Oddbods such as Fuse, Bubbles, Pogo, Zee, Slick, Jeff and Newt.
  • Fun Oddbods theme song
  • Each Oddbod brings something interesting to the game
  • Over 60 levels more more to be added soon
  • Interactive tutorial

Fans of soccer or football will love this game. If you’re into the World Cup, English Premier League or UEFA Champions League, this game will be interesting too.
