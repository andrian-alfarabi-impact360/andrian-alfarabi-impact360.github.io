Play mini games featuring the characters from Dumb Ways to Die.

Dumb Ways to Die is a worldwide brand with over 300 million downloads on Apple App Store and Google Play combined.

Test your reflexes in this challenging series of mini games, where a milisecond can make the difference between winning and losing.

Score coins for each challenge, and use the to repair the once great town of Dumbville.

Features:

  • Extremely challenging levels.
  • Play as your favorite Beans: Stupe, Lax, Dippy, Dummkopf and Stumble,
  • Soar through the skies above Dumbville in a crazy plane.
  • Dash through the mighty Pyramids of Dumb-Gypt.
  • Collect unique outfits for all the Beans!
  • Explore a space station in Shuttle shift.
  • Send the Beans down the steep slopes of Dumb Peak.
