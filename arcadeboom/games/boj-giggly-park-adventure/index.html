Explore Giggly Park with Boj and friends.

Boj is an award winning kids television series worldwide.

Do wonderful things such as ride a bike, climb the tree, fly a kite, dig underground for treasures, ride the swing, control a helicopter remotely and more.

Interact with various characters from the Boj universe, such as Mimi, Pops, Mr Cloppity, Denzil, Mia Twitch and more.

This is a new and innovative gameplay to encourage users to explore their curiosity. Almost everything in the screen can be interacted with. Tap or drag and drop various objects in the screen.

Features:

  • No time limit
  • No limit to replayability. Literally spend hours exploring this game with your child.
  • Fun and positive theme to encourage creativity and self exploration
  • Great graphics courtesy of Boj, a great kids animation brand.
  • Great audio from the Boj TV series
