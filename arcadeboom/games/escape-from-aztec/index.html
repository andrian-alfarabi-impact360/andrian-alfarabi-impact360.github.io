You just stole King Aztec’s greatest treasure. Now you’re being hunted.

Run as fast as you can. Avoid tree trunks, boulders and ancient ruins. Switch lanes, jump and slide your way to freedom.

Collect treasures along the way to purchase powerups.

How far can you escape?

Features:

  • 3D-like environment built with Aztec theme
  • Use Aztec coins to purchase powerups in the store
  • Use a variety of powerups such as invincibility, coin magnets, megaboost and extra lives
